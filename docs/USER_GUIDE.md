## What is Ambira?

Ambira is a social productivity tracking application that gamifies your work sessions. Just like Strava tracks runs and bike rides, Ambira tracks your productive work sessions - whether it's coding, studying, reading, planning, or any activity you choose.

**Key Benefits:**

- **Stay Accountable**: Share your productivity with friends and followers
- **Build Streaks**: Track consecutive days of productive work
- **Join Challenges**: Compete in productivity challenges with groups and friends
- **Gain Insights**: Visualize your productivity patterns with analytics
- **Customize Activities**: Track work across 10 default activities or create your own custom activities

**How It Works:**

1. Track work sessions using the built-in timer or manual entry
2. Sessions appear in your profile and your followers' feeds
3. Earn support (likes) and comments from your network
4. Build daily streaks and unlock achievements
5. Join groups and challenges to stay motivated

## Getting Started

### Signing Up

1. **Visit Ambira**: Navigate to [ambira.app](https://www.ambira.app) in your web browser
2. **Create Account**: Click "Sign Up" and choose your method:
   - Email and password
   - Google account (one-click sign-up)
3. **Set Up Profile**: Add your name, username, bio, and profile picture
4. **Choose Activities**: Ambira provides 10 default activities (Work, Coding, Side Project, Planning, Study, Learning, Reading, Research, Creative, Writing)

### First Session

1. **Navigate to Timer**: Click the timer icon in the header or bottom navigation
2. **Select Activity**: Choose what you're working on (e.g., "Coding")
3. **Start Timer**: Click "Start" to begin tracking your session
4. **Work Session**: Focus on your task while the timer runs
5. **Finish Session**: When done, click "Finish" to save your session
6. **Add Details**: Optionally add a title, notes, and visibility settings
7. **Share**: Your session appears in your profile and followers' feeds

## Core Features

### Tracking Sessions with the Timer

The session timer is the heart of Ambira. It tracks your productive work time and creates sessions that appear in the social feed.

#### Starting a Session

1. **Access Timer**: Click the timer icon (â±ï¸) in the header or navigation
2. **Select Activity**: Choose from:
   - **Recent Activities**: Your 5 most recently used activities (shown in horizontal bar)
   - **All Activities**: Complete list of system defaults and your custom activities
3. **Start Tracking**: Click "Start" to begin
4. **Timer Display**: See elapsed time in header (shows everywhere except timer page)

#### During a Session

- **Pause/Resume**: Pause the timer if you need a break, resume when ready
- **Active Indicator**: Header shows "Active" when you're on the timer page, or live elapsed time on other pages
- **Background Tracking**: Timer continues running even if you navigate to other pages or close the browser
- **Persistence**: Your active session is saved automatically and restored if you return later

#### Finishing a Session

1. **Click Finish**: Stop the timer when your work session is complete
2. **Add Details** (modal appears):
   - **Title**: Give your session a name (e.g., "Built login feature")
   - **Notes**: Add details about what you accomplished
   - **Activity**: Confirm or change the activity type
   - **Visibility**: Choose who can see this session:
     - **Everyone**: Public - appears in everyone's feed
     - **Followers**: Only people who follow you can see
     - **Private**: Only visible to you
3. **Save**: Click "Save Session" to post

#### Manual Session Entry

If you forgot to start the timer, you can log sessions manually:

1. **Manual Entry**: Click "Log Manual" on the timer page
2. **Enter Details**:
   - Date and time
   - Duration
   - Activity type
   - Title and notes
3. **Save**: Manual sessions appear in your profile just like timed sessions

### Challenges

Compete with friends and groups in productivity challenges. Test yourself and stay motivated through friendly competition.

#### Challenge Types

Ambira offers four types of challenges:

1. **Most Activity**: Compete to log the most productive hours
   - Goal: Accumulate the most total time
   - Leaderboard ranks by hours logged
   - Example: "100 Hours in November"

2. **Fastest Effort**: Achieve the best tasks-per-hour ratio
   - Goal: Maximize productivity efficiency
   - Measures completed tasks per hour
   - Example: "Sprint Week - Highest Velocity"

3. **Longest Session**: Record the longest single work session
   - Goal: Single longest continuous session
   - Deep work and focus challenge
   - Example: "Deep Work Saturday - 4+ Hours"

4. **Group Goal**: Work together to reach a collective target
   - Goal: Combined team effort
   - All participants contribute to shared goal
   - Example: "Team 500 - Log 500 hours together"

#### Discovering Challenges

1. **Challenges Page**: Navigate to Challenges in navigation
2. **Browse Challenges**:
   - Active challenges (currently running)
   - Upcoming challenges (not yet started)

3. **Right Sidebar**: Quick view of active challenges you're in
4. **Group Challenges**: View in Groups â†’ [Group Name] â†’ Challenges tab

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is Ambira free to use?**
A: Yes! Ambira is free to use. Create an account and start tracking your productivity today.

**Q: Do I need to download an app?**
A: No. Ambira is a web application that works in your browser. Simply visit ambira.app to get started. A mobile app may be available in the future.

**Q: Can I use Ambira offline?**
A: Active sessions are saved locally and sync when you're back online. However, viewing the feed and social features requires an internet connection.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Ambira works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

### Account and Privacy

**Q: How do I change my username?**
A: Navigate to Settings â†’ My Account to view your username. Contact support if you need to change it.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes. Go to Settings â†’ My Account â†’ Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

**Q: Who can see my sessions?**
A: It depends on your session visibility setting:

- **Everyone**: Public - visible in global feed
- **Followers**: Only people who follow you
- **Private**: Only you can see it

You can set default visibility in Settings â†’ Privacy, or choose visibility per session when saving.

**Q: Can I make my entire profile private?**
A: Yes. Go to Settings â†’ Privacy â†’ Profile Visibility â†’ Private. Only you will be able to view your profile.

### Sessions and Tracking

**Q: What happens if I forget to start the timer?**
A: Use the "Log Manual" feature to record past sessions. Enter the date, time, and duration manually.

**Q: Can I edit a session after saving it?**
A: Yes. Go to your Profile â†’ Sessions, find the session, and click "Edit" to modify details.

**Q: Can I delete a session?**
A: Yes. Navigate to the session and click "Delete". This action cannot be undone.

**Q: Does the timer keep running if I close my browser?**
A: Yes! Your active session persists and will resume when you return to Ambira.

**Q: Can I pause a timer?**
A: Yes. Click "Pause" during an active session. Click "Resume" to continue timing.

**Q: What's the maximum session duration?**
A: There's no hard limit, but sessions over 12 hours may be flagged for review to prevent errors.

### Activities

**Q: Can I track activities that aren't in the default list?**
A: Yes! Create custom activities in Settings â†’ Activities. You can create up to 10 custom activities.

**Q: Can I delete default activities?**
A: No. The 10 default activities are system-wide and cannot be deleted. However, you don't have to use them.

**Q: How many custom activities can I create?**
A: You can create up to 10 custom activities per account.

**Q: Can I edit a custom activity after creating it?**
A: Yes. Go to Settings â†’ Activities, find your custom activity, and click "Edit".

### Social Features

**Q: How do I find friends on Ambira?**
A: Navigate to Users â†’ Search. Search by name or username to find friends.

**Q: What does "Support" mean?**
A: Supporting a session is like "liking" it - it shows appreciation and encouragement for someone's productivity.

**Q: Can I see who supported my session?**
A: Yes. Click on the support count to see a list of users who supported your session.

**Q: How do I remove a follower?**
A: You cannot directly remove followers, but you can set your profile to private to restrict who can follow you.

**Q: Can I block users?**
A: Blocking functionality is coming soon. For now, set your profile to private or report inappropriate behavior to support.

### Groups and Challenges

**Q: How do I leave a group?**
A: Navigate to the group page and click "Leave Group".

**Q: Can I create a challenge without a group?**
A: Currently, challenges are group-specific. Create a group first, then create challenges within that group.

**Q: What activities count toward a challenge?**
A: When creating a challenge, the admin selects which activities are eligible. Check the challenge details to see what counts.

**Q: Can I join a challenge after it has started?**
A: Yes, but only progress after you join will count toward your total.

**Q: What happens if I leave a group during a challenge?**
A: You'll be removed from any active challenges associated with that group.

## Troubleshooting

### Login and Authentication Issues

**Problem: Can't log in with email/password**

- Verify your email and password are correct
- Check for typos in email address
- Try "Forgot Password" to reset
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

**Problem: Google sign-in not working**

- Ensure you're using the correct Google account
- Check that cookies are enabled
- Allow pop-ups for ambira.app
- Try signing out of Google and back in
- Use incognito/private browsing mode to test

**Problem: "Invalid credentials" error**

- Double-check your password
- Use "Forgot Password" if you're unsure
- Ensure Caps Lock is off
- Try copying and pasting password to rule out typing errors

### Timer Issues

**Problem: Timer not starting**

- Refresh the page
- Check that you selected an activity
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check browser console for errors (F12 â†’ Console)

**Problem: Timer disappeared after refreshing**

- Active session should auto-restore
- Check header for active session indicator
- Navigate to /timer page directly
- If session is lost, use "Log Manual" to recreate

**Problem: Timer shows wrong elapsed time**

- Check your device's system time
- Refresh the page to resync
- If time is significantly wrong, finish and log manually with correct duration

**Problem: Can't pause/resume timer**

- Refresh the page
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Try logging out and back in

### Session Saving Issues

**Problem: "Error saving session" message**

- Check your internet connection
- Verify all required fields are filled
- Try refreshing and saving again
- Check that session duration is reasonable (> 0 minutes)
- Clear browser cache if persistent

**Problem: Session saved but not showing in feed**

- Check session visibility setting (might be private)
- Refresh the feed
- Check your profile to verify session was saved
- If visibility is "Followers" and you have no followers, it won't appear in public feed

**Problem: Can't edit saved session**

- Refresh the page and try again
- Ensure you own the session (can only edit your own)
- Check internet connection
- Try from profile page instead of feed

### Feed and Social Issues

**Problem: Feed not loading**

- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try logging out and back in
- Check Firestore indexes (see Firebase Setup docs)

**Problem: Following feed is empty**

- Verify you're following users (go to Users page)
- Check that people you follow have public or followers-visible sessions
- Refresh the feed
- Try switching to "Recent" feed to test

**Problem: Can't follow/unfollow users**

- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Verify user's profile visibility settings

**Problem: Comments not appearing**

- Refresh the page
- Check that comments were actually submitted (look for success message)
- Verify internet connection during submission
- Try posting comment again

### Profile and Settings Issues

**Problem: Profile picture not updating**

- Ensure image is under 5MB
- Use supported formats (JPG, PNG, GIF)
- Check internet connection during upload
- Try a different image
- Clear browser cache after upload

**Problem: Changes not saving in settings**

- Click "Save" button after making changes
- Check for error messages
- Verify internet connection
- Refresh page to see if changes persisted
- Try different browser if persistent

**Problem: Can't see other user's profile**

- Check their privacy settings (may be private or followers-only)
- Verify you're logged in
- Try refreshing the page
- Ensure username is spelled correctly in URL

### Activity Issues

**Problem: Custom activity not appearing**

- Refresh the page
- Check that activity was saved (go to Settings â†’ Activities)
- Verify you haven't reached the 10 custom activity limit
- Clear browser cache

**Problem: Can't delete custom activity**

- Ensure no active sessions are using that activity
- Refresh the page
- Try from Settings â†’ Activities page
- Check internet connection

**Problem: Recent activities not showing**

- Log at least one session with an activity
- Recent activities appear after first use
- Refresh the page to see updated recent list

### Challenge Issues

**Problem: Not seeing challenge progress**

- Verify sessions use eligible activities for the challenge
- Check that sessions were logged during challenge timeframe
- Refresh the leaderboard
- Ensure you successfully joined the challenge

**Problem: Can't join challenge**

- Check challenge start/end dates
- Verify you're a member of the associated group
- Refresh the page
- Try logging out and back in

**Problem: Leaderboard not updating**

- Progress updates may take a few moments
- Refresh the page
- Check that your sessions match eligible activities
- Verify sessions were saved successfully

### Performance Issues

**Problem: App is slow or unresponsive**

- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Close unnecessary browser tabs
- Check internet connection speed
- Try different browser
- Restart browser
- Check device storage (ensure not full)

**Problem: Images not loading**

- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Disable browser extensions temporarily

### General Error Messages

**Problem: "Something went wrong" error**

- Refresh the page
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Try logging out and back in
- Check browser console for specific errors (F12 â†’ Console)
- Take screenshot and report to support if persistent

**Problem: Firestore index error**

- Click the provided link in error message to auto-create index
- Wait 5-10 minutes for index to build
- Refresh page after index builds
- See Firebase Setup docs for manual index creation

**Problem: "Permission denied" error**

- Verify you're logged in
- Check that you have permission for the action (e.g., can't edit other user's sessions)
- Try logging out and back in
- Refresh page
- Contact support if you should have access
